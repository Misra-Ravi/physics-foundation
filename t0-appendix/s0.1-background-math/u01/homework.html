Rules Reminder

  1. The farthest-left non-zero digit is the most significant figure.
  2. No decimal point → farthest-right non-zero digit is the least significant figure.
  3. Decimal point present → farthest-right digit is least significant — even if it is zero.
  4. All digits between most and least significant are significant.
Scientific notation tip: Only count digits in the coefficient. The power of 10 does not affect significant figures.
