Velocity-Time Graphs Part 2
Graphs of Changing Velocity

Key Formulae — VT Graphs

a = ΔV / Δt
Area (▲) = ½ × b × h
Area (▭) = b × h
Y-intercept = U (initial V)
Slope = acceleration
Area under graph = displacement

1 A runner completes a race with the following VT profile:
• Phase 1: Accelerates from rest to +8 ms⁻¹ in 4 s

(b) After how many seconds does Object B have the same velocity as Object A?
